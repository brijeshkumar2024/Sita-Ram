Our History


Mohanty Mart was founded by Mr. Mahesh Kumar Mohanty, a dedicated advocate for natural wellness with over 18 years of experience in Ayurveda. Established in 2012, the journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to provide people with easy access to high-quality, natural products that promote overall well-being.

In the early days, Mohanty Mart started as a small local store , With limited resources but abundant passion, Mahesh focused on curating a select range of Ayurvedic medicines, herbal remedies, and personal care products. His extensive knowledge and commitment to quality quickly garnered the trust of the local community, establishing Mohanty Mart as a go-to destination for natural wellness solutions.

As our reputation grew, so did our product offerings. Recognizing the diverse needs of our customers, we expanded our inventory to include a wide variety of categories such as snacks, beverages, beauty products, household essentials, stationery, and more. Each new addition was carefully chosen to align with our mission of promoting health and well-being.

In 2024, we launched our online platform, making it easier for customers to explore our offerings from the comfort of their homes. This significant step allowed us to reach a broader audience, providing access to our quality products beyond local boundaries.

Throughout our journey, our commitment to quality has remained unwavering. Under Mahesh's leadership, we prioritize sourcing products from trusted suppliers who share our values of sustainability and ethical practices. Our rigorous quality control measures ensure that every item on our shelves meets the highest standards, allowing our customers to shop with confidence.

At Mohanty Mart, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us. Over the years, we have participated in various local initiatives, wellness programs, and educational workshops to raise awareness about health, nutrition, and sustainable living. Our goal is not only to provide products but also to foster a community that values well-being and healthy lifestyles.

As we continue to grow and evolve, our mission remains steadfast: to be your trusted source for natural wellness and quality living. We are excited about the future and committed to expanding our offerings while maintaining the same principles that have guided us from the beginning.

Thank you for being a part of our journey.
We look forward to serving you and contributing to your wellness journey for years to come!
